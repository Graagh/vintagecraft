==== Smoke and Gasses ====

- Smoke generated from fires, bloomeries, etc. dissipate only when there is access to sky. Otherwise it only dissipates slowly. 

- Exposure to smoke makes players caugh occassionally

- Long expore to smoke adds nausea effect
